About

The main purpose behind Welcome Wellness is to enhance the day to day life of people especially young minds. We provide people with A.I. powered tools to help them measure, control and focus on their wellbeing, the smart way. Our A.I. tools have been trained on extensive data use to help you track your health.

Our Process

Our website consistes of 4 main functionalities which are BMI calculator, BMR checker, Stress predictor, Diabetes predictor.

Our Approach

BMI calculator helps you calculate your Body Mass Index by taking input as AGE, Height, Weight, Gender. BMR checker calculates number of calories you are supposed to eat based on your BMI. Stress predictor helps you predict the amount of your stress level. Diabetes predictor helps you predict that if you are going to have diabetes or not based on your deit.

Our Goal

Our goal is to ensure that people prioritise their health over any anything else in this universe even Aliens.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make sure that people are acknowledging their health and keeping themselves Well and Fit.
